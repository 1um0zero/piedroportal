# Launch announcement + first-login instructions

Ready-to-send copy for the email broadcast tool (/admin/email). Paste the EN
version as the base, then use the per-language tabs (NL/FR/DE) — each recipient
receives their own language automatically. Replace links if the domain differs.

Portal: **https://portal.piedro.pt**

---

## 🇬🇧 English

**Subject:** Your new Piedro ordering portal is live

Dear {{name}},

We are happy to welcome you to the **new Piedro ordering portal** — faster,
multilingual, and always one step ahead.

**How to log in the first time**
1. Go to **https://portal.piedro.pt** and click **Login**.
2. Enter your email address (the one you are reading this on).
3. You will be offered a one-time setup link — we email it to you.
4. Open the link and choose your own password.
5. That's it — you're in. Your previous orders are already there.

If you have any questions, the in-portal assistant (bottom-right) can help, or
simply reply to this email.

Kind regards,
The Piedro team
